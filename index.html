How we roast & source

We keep our process simple and transparent – so you always know what’s in your cup.

BeanCraft Coffee is a small roastery focused on specialty-grade Arabica beans. We work with importers and small farms that care about fair pay, careful processing, and long-term relationships.

Every batch is roasted to highlight the character of its origin. No “one roast fits all” approach, no burnt flavors – just clean, honest coffee that actually tastes like coffee.

  • ✓
    Only specialty-grade Arabica beans, cupped and scored before we buy.
  • ✓
    Roasted in small batches and rested for 24–72 hours before shipping.
  • ✓
    Clear information on origin, process, and flavor notes on every bag.
Perfect for home brewers From simple French press to V60, Aeropress, Chemex, our light and medium roasts are designed to be easy to dial in at home, without a professional machine.
Wholesale & coffee shops We also roast larger batches for cafés, offices, and small businesses. Consistent profiles, easy communication, and support with brew recipes.
Freshness first We never ship coffee that’s been sitting on a shelf for months. Your beans are roasted shortly before we pack and send them out.
Sustainable choices Our bags are recyclable where facilities exist, and we prioritize partners who focus on environmental and social responsibility at origin.
